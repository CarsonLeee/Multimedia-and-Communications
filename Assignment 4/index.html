Who are we?

Founded in 1989 by michelin chef Carson Lee who immmigrated to Canada and has worked along side many of the top chefs around the world. He decided to start his own BBQ restaurant because of his love and passion for cooking. We are among the top BBQ restaurants in the country and have won many cook-off tournaments. Feel free to call us and book a reservation for an experience of a lifetime.
